# California Notice of Financial Incentive

## 1. Overview

This California Notice of Financial Incentive is provided pursuant to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and applies solely to residents of the State of California.

## 2. No Financial Incentive Programs

Catch AI does not offer any financial incentives, compensation, discounts, rewards, loyalty programs, referral benefits, or other value-based programs in exchange for the collection, retention, use, or sharing of personal information or biometric data.

## 3. Personal Information and Service Access

- Personal information is collected only to the extent necessary to provide the Catch AI service, comply with applicable laws, and ensure service security.
- Users are not provided with monetary or non-monetary benefits in exchange for providing personal information.

## 4. Non-Discrimination

In accordance with the CCPA and CPRA, Catch AI does not discriminate against users for exercising their privacy rights, including the right to access, delete, or limit the use of personal information.

## 5. Future Changes

If Catch AI introduces any financial incentive programs in the future, we will:

- Provide prior and clear notice of the program
- Disclose the categories of personal information involved
- Explain how the value of the incentive is reasonably related to the value of the data
- Obtain user consent where required by law

## 6. Consumer Rights Under California Law

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected and used
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information
- Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

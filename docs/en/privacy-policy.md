# 개인정보 처리방침 (Privacy Policy)

**Last updated**: 2026년 02월 03일

AISRC (hereinafter referred to as the “Company”) establishes and discloses this Privacy Policy in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act, the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, the Enforcement Decree of the Personal Information Protection Act, and Google Play policies, in order to protect users’ personal information and biometric data and to promptly handle related complaints.

This Privacy Policy explains how and for what purposes users’ personal information is processed in connection with the use of the AI-based deepfake voice detection application “Catch AI” (hereinafter referred to as the “Service”) provided by the Company.

## 1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information and Biometric Data

The Company processes personal information and biometric data solely for the following purposes:

- Provision of AI-based deepfake voice detection services
- Detection of deepfake content in audio files uploaded by users from SNS platforms, messaging services, and external storage
- Detection of deepfake content in audio recorded directly by users through the Service
- Service quality improvement, error analysis, and security response
- Compliance with applicable laws and Google Play policies

※ The Company does not use personal information for purposes other than those stated above.

## 2. Categories of Personal Information Processed and Methods of Collection

### (1) Categories of Personal Information Processed

#### A. Mandatory Information

**Account Information**

- Email address provided through Google Sign-In
- Google account identifier used for internal authentication purposes

**Audio Data**

- Audio files directly uploaded by users to the Service
- Audio files selected and imported by users from external sources such as SNS platforms, messaging services, email, or cloud storage
- Audio recorded directly by users using the in-app recording function

※ Such audio data may constitute biometric information under the Personal Information Protection Act if it can be used to identify an individual.

**Device and Service Usage Information**

- Operating system (OS) information and app version
- De-identified device information
- Detection request history and error logs

#### B. Information Not Processed

The Company does not collect or process the following information:

- Name, resident registration number, address, or phone number
- Text messages or call logs
- Location information
- Other biometric information such as fingerprints or facial data

### (2) Methods of Collection

- Login through Google accounts
- Upload of audio files directly by users
- Selection and import of audio files by users from external services (SNS, messaging apps, etc.)
- Direct audio recording using the Service’s recording function
- Automatically generated device information and log data during service use

## 3. Processing of Biometric Information (Audio Data)

- Audio data is used exclusively for automated AI-based analysis for deepfake detection purposes.
- The Company does not store audio data on its servers as a general rule, and audio data is immediately deleted after analysis is completed.
- Audio data is not used for AI model training, reuse, re-identification, marketing, or provision to third parties.
- Users are solely responsible for ensuring that they have the lawful right to collect and use any audio data they upload or record.
- If audio data must be retained in accordance with applicable laws, the Company will notify users in advance of the items retained, retention period, and reasons for retention.

## 4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

- The Company promptly destroys personal information and biometric data once the purpose of processing has been achieved.
- However, if retention is required under applicable laws, such information may be retained for the period prescribed by such laws.

## 5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

As a general rule, the Company does not provide users’ personal information or biometric data to third parties. Exceptions apply only in the following cases:

- Where the user has given prior consent
- Where provision is required by applicable laws or regulations

## 6. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

The Company does not outsource personal information processing tasks to third parties. If outsourcing becomes necessary in the future, the Company will disclose the outsourced tasks and service providers in advance through this Privacy Policy.

## 7. Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

- Request access to personal information
- Request correction or deletion of personal information
- Request suspension of personal information processing

Requests may be submitted in writing or by email, and the Company will take appropriate measures without delay in accordance with applicable laws.

## 8. Procedures and Methods for Destruction of Personal Information

### (1) Destruction Procedure

Personal information is destroyed immediately after the processing purpose is achieved, in accordance with internal management procedures.

### (2) Destruction Method

Electronic files are deleted using technical methods that make recovery impossible.

## 9. Measures to Ensure the Security of Personal Information

The Company implements the following measures to ensure the security of personal information and biometric data:

- Minimization and management of access rights to personal information
- Access control to personal information processing systems
- Regular security vulnerability assessments and appropriate remediation measures  
- Periodic security training for personnel handling personal information

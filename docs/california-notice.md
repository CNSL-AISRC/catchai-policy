# California Notice of Financial Incentive

**Last Updated: January 22, 2026**

## 1. Overview

This notice is provided pursuant to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and applies to California residents. It describes our financial incentive programs related to the collection and use of personal information.

## 2. Financial Incentive Program

### 2.1 Program Description

CatchAI may offer customers financial incentives, discounts, and other benefits in exchange for the collection and retention of personal data or participation in data collection activities. These incentives are entirely voluntary.

### 2.2 Incentive Examples

- Discount codes for providing email address
- Loyalty rewards for account information
- Referral bonuses for customer referrals
- Special offers for data sharing with selected partners
- Cashback rewards for purchases

## 3. Personal Information Involved

The financial incentive programs may involve the collection and use of:

- Name and contact information
- Purchase history and preferences
- Device identifiers
- Browsing and usage data
- Demographic information
- Location information (if applicable)

## 4. Estimated Value of Incentive

The financial value of incentives is determined based on:

- Direct costs of providing the incentive
- Costs of administering the program
- Value of consumer data collected
- Potential value to be derived from analysis

The estimated value of each incentive will be clearly disclosed at the time of offer.

## 5. Participation and Withdrawal

### 5.1 How to Participate

Customers may opt-in to financial incentive programs through:

- Explicit consent on our website
- Signing up through our application
- Accepting terms at point of sale
- Completing registration forms

### 5.2 How to Withdraw

Customers may withdraw from a financial incentive program at any time by:

- Emailing privacy@aisrc.net
- Accessing account settings and opting out
- Calling our customer service line
- Submitting a written request

Withdrawal is effective immediately and does not require advance notice.

## 6. Impact of Non-Participation or Withdrawal

### 6.1 Rights

California law prohibits discrimination against customers for exercising their rights, including:

- Refusing to participate in incentive programs
- Opting out of incentive programs
- Exercising CCPA rights

### 6.2 Discrimination Prohibition

We will not:

- Deny goods or services based on participation status
- Charge different prices or rates
- Provide different quality of service or goods
- Suggest that non-participation will adversely affect the consumer

The financial incentive reflects the reasonable value of consumer data as permitted by law.

## 7. Data Usage and Sharing

Personal information collected through financial incentive programs will be:

- Used consistent with this notice and our Privacy Policy
- Retained for program administration and legal requirements
- Protected with appropriate security measures
- Shared only with service providers and as required by law

## 8. Program Changes

CatchAI reserves the right to:

- Modify, suspend, or terminate incentive programs
- Change incentive values and terms
- Add new programs or remove existing ones

Notice of material changes will be provided at least 30 days in advance.
